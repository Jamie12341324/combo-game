Fantasy battle game

Instructions

Create combos by choosing 2 numbers and summoning them using money. Gain money by defeating enemies and bosses. After each battle allied combos respawn. Each combo has a power level after 2 combos battle the combo with the higher power level defeats the opponent and the higher power level one has its power level reduced by its opponents power level until the combos respawn after the battles. To find enemies click on either Enemy, Boss1 or Boss 2 at the top left on the navbar.
